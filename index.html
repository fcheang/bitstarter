Yard Sale Near By is a site that helps yard sale host to let people know they have yard sale going on. It also help people like to go to yard sale to find out where to find yard sale.

You can put only so manay yard sale sign on the road. What if you can tell more people that you have a yard sale going on. Just go to the site and enter the date/time/lcoation of your yard sale. People near by, either happens to drive by or live close by, can see it with their smart phone. For people who love to go to yard sale, they can find all the yard sales that are happening at any location. So you no longer need to look for Yard Sale sign. At the tip of your finger you can see all close by yard sale event.
